Claim your State Pension

You’ll get a letter no later than 2 months before you reach State Pension age inviting you to make a claim.

When you get the letter, you can get your State Pension online or you can call us.

Make a claim

Tell us about a change of circumstances or ask about your State Pension

If you’re already getting your State Pension, you can contact the Pension Service to:

  • report a change in your circumstances, such as a change of address or bank details
  • tell us about someone who has died
  • ask about your existing State Pension

Telephone: 0800 731 0469
Textphone: 0800 731 0464

Get a State Pension forecast

If you have not reached State Pension age, you can find out how much you could get by:

  • using the check your State Pension online service
  • contacting the Future Pension Centre

Contact the Pension Service if you live overseas

If you live abroad, to make a claim for State Pension, tell us about a change of circumstances or get information about your State Pension, contact the International Pension Centre.

Write to or complain about the Pension Service

If you want to write to the Pension Service, find your local pension centre centre using your postcode to get their contact details.

Contact the Department for Work and Pensions to complain about the Pension Service.
