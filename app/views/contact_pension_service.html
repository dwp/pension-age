Making a new claim for the State Pension

You’ll get a letter no later than 2 months before you reach State Pension age.

Once you get the letter, you can put in a claim for your State Pension online or by phoning the claim line.

Tell us about a change of circumstances or if someone has died

If you’re already getting your State Pension, or if you have delayed (deferred) claiming it, you can contact the Pension Service to report a change in your circumstances, such as a change of address or bank details. You can also contact us to report a death.

You should only tell us about a change of address after you have moved.
