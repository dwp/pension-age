The earliest you can get your State Pension is 1 November 2032, when you'll be 66

View a forecast of how much State Pension you could get.

You won’t get your State Pension automatically - you’ll have to claim it. You should receive a letter up to 2 months before you reach State Pension age telling you how to do this.

Other financial support you get at State Pension age

  • Winter Fuel Payments - you get these automatically when you claim your State Pension to help with your heating bill
  • Bus pass - apply for free travel
  • Pension Credit - if you're on a low income
