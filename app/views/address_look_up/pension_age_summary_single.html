Support you could get at State Pension age

You reach State Pension age on 6 July 2019

State Pension

We'll send you a letter up to 2 months before this date telling you how to claim your State Pension.

You can also check your State Pension to find out how much you could get.

Free bus pass

When you reach State Pension age you can get apply to get a bus pass for free travel in England.

More on the free bus pass

Winter Fuel Payment

When you reach State Pension age and you live on your own you could get a Winter Fuel Payment of £200 every year.

You get these automatically if you're getting State Pension.

More on Winter Fuel Payment

Pension Credit

When you reach State Pension age and you live on your own you could get Pension Credit to top up your State Pension to £167.25 a week.

How much Pension Credit you get depends on:

  • your joint income being less than £167.25 a week
  • the amount in savings and property you have
  • if you have a disability
  • if you're a carer

If you’re eligible, Pension Credit could also help you get other benefits too. For example, reduced Council Tax, Housing Benefit to help pay your rent and free prescriptions.

You have to claim Pension Credit.

More on Pension Credit
