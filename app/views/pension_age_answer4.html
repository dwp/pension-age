When you can get your State Pension

You'll reach State Pension age on 19 September 2056.

On this date you'll be 68 years old.

The State Pension age is under review and may change in the future.

You can check your State Pension forecast to find out how much you could get.

What else you could get

When you reach State Pension age, you may also be eligible for:

  • Winter Fuel Payment - you'll automatically get this if you're getting State Pension and live in the UK
  • Pension Credit - if you're on a low income, you may be able to get this
  • a bus pass - the age you can apply depends on where you live in the UK
