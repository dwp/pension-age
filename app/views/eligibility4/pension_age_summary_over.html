Because you reached State Pension age on 6 August 2019 you can apply for the following:

State Pension

You can get your State Pension now if you haven't already claimed it.

Get your State Pension

Free bus pass

You can apply to get a bus pass for free travel in England.

Apply for a free bus pass

Pension Credit

You could get Pension Credit to top up your State Pension to £255.25 a week. To qualify, your partner must have also have reached State Pension age. Or one of you is over State Pension age and getting Housing Benefit.

How much Pension Credit you get depends on:

  • your joint income being less than £255.55 a week
  • the amount in savings and property you have
  • if you have a disability
  • if you're a carer

If you’re eligible, Pension Credit could also help you get other benefits too. For example, reduced Council Tax, Housing Benefit to help pay your rent and free prescriptions.

You have to claim Pension Credit.

Apply for Pension Credit

Winter Fuel Payment

You could get a payment to help with your heating bill of

  • £100 every year, or
  • £200 every year if your partner doesn't qualify for Winter Fuel or they're on certain benefits

You should get Winter Fuel Payment automatically if you're already getting your State Pension or Pension Credit.
