Support you could get at State Pension age4

You reach State Pension age on 6 July 2022

On this date you'll be 66 years old.

State Pension

You'll need to claim your State Pension, you must be within 4 months of your State Pension age to do so.

You can also check your State Pension to find out how much you could get.

Winter Fuel Payment

Even if you live overseas when you reach State Pension age you may be eligible for a Winter Fuel Payment if both the following apply:

  • you live in Switzerland or a European Economic Area (EEA) country
  • you have a genuine link to the UK - this is decided on an individual basis, and can include personal and family circumstances

You can not get the payment if you live in Cyprus, France, Gibraltar, Greece, Malta, Portugal or Spain because the average winter temperature is higher than the warmest region of the UK.

You will need to apply to get Winter Fuel Payment.

More on Winter Fuel Payment

Support you can not get

Free bus pass

You cannot get a UK free bus pass if you live permanently overseas.

Pension Credit

You cannot get Pension Credit if you live permanently overseas.
