You can get your State Pension now

You reached State Pension age on 5 March 2019, when you turned 66.

What to do next

Because you have reached State Pension age, you can choose to either:

  • make a claim to get your State Pension
  • delay getting your State Pension - you may get more State Pension when you claim it

What else you could get

Because you have reached State Pension age you may also be eligible for:

  • Winter Fuel Payment - you'll automatically get this if you're getting State Pension and live in the UK
  • Pension Credit - if you're on a low income, you may be able to get this
  • a bus pass - the age you can apply depends on where you live in the UK
