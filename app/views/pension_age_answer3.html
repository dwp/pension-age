When you can get your State Pension

You'll reach State Pension age on 6 December 2019.

On this date you'll be 66 years and 3 months old.

What happens next

We'll send you a letter soon telling you how to claim your State Pension and when you'll get your first payment. When you get it you can:

  • make a claim to get your State Pension
  • delay getting your State Pension - you may get more State Pension when you come to claim it

You can also check your State Pension forecast to find out how much you could get.
