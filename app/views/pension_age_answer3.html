When you can get your State Pension

You'll reach State Pension age on 6 May 2019.

On this date you'll be 66 years and 3 months old.

What happens next

We'll send you a letter soon telling you how to claim your State Pension. When you get it you can:

  • make a claim to get your State Pension
  • delay getting your State Pension - you may get more State Pension when you come to claim it

You can also check your State Pension to find out how much you could get.

What else you could get

When you reach State Pension age, you may also be eligible for:

  • Winter Fuel Payment - you'll automatically get this if you're getting State Pension
  • Pension Credit - if you're on a low income, you may be able to get this
  • a bus pass - the age you can apply depends on where you live in the UK
