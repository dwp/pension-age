Because you live on your own

When you reach State Pension age on 6 July 2019.

You could get a Winter Fuel Payment of

  • £200 every year

You get these automatically if you're getting State Pension.

More on Winter Fuel Payment

You could get Pension Credit

Pension credit could top up your State Pension to £167.25 a week.

How much Pension Credit you get depends on:

  • your income being less than £167.25 a week
  • the amount in savings and property you have
  • if you have a disability
  • if you're a carer

If you’re eligible, Pension Credit could also help you get other benefits too. For example, reduced Council Tax, Housing Benefit to help pay your rent and free prescriptions.

You have to claim Pension Credit.
