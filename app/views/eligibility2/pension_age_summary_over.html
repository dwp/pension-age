You can apply now

Because you reached State Pension age on 6 August 2019 you can apply for the following:

State Pension

You can get your State Pension now if you haven't already claimed it.

Free bus pass

You can apply to get a bus pass for free travel in England.

Winter Fuel Payment

You could get a payment of £100 every year to help with your heating bill.

Pension Credit

You could get Pension Credit to top up your State Pension to £255.25 a week if your income as a couple if less than that.

If you’re eligible, Pension Credit could also help you get other benefits too. For example, reduced Council Tax, Housing Benefit to help pay your rent and free medical treatments.
