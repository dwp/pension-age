Overview

If you were born on or before 5 April 1954, you’ll normally get a Winter Fuel Payment automatically to help you pay your heating bills.

This guide is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

If you’re eligible, your payment will be between £100 and £300 depending on your circumstances.

Most payments are made during November and December.

Contact the Winter Fuel Payment Centre if you do not get your payment by 13 January 2020.

Any money you get will not affect your other benefits.

Some people will still need to make a claim under certain circumstances, but should only need to claim it once.
