Overview

If you were born on or before 5 April 1954 you could get between £100 and £300 to help you pay your heating bills. This is known as a ‘Winter Fuel Payment’.

You usually get a Winter Fuel Payment automatically every year if you are eligible and you get the State Pension or another benefit, such as Pension Credit (but not Housing Benefit, Council Tax Reduction, Child Benefit or Universal Credit).

You’ll get a letter telling you how much you will get and an estimated payment date.

Most payments are made automatically between November and December. You should get your money by 13 January 2020.

Find out what to do if you don't get paid automatically or have a problem with your payment.

Any money you get will not affect your other benefits.
